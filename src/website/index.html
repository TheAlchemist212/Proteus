The easy way to analyze water samples.


What it is:

Proteus is a revolutionary new way to test water samples through an AI recognition system that we created. It can test water samples given to it accurately, and you can get the images by using a cell phone microscope attachment. To see if the images contain any bacteria, upload the image into our image scanning software.


Our Product:



Interactive 3D Model - powered by EchoAR
Our microscope attachment contains an objective lens, eye/camera piece, clamps, and movable rods for your phone or camera.
Our attachment will be made out of high quality stainless steel and durable plastic.
